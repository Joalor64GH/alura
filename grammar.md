## Word Order
This conlang uses OSV (Object-Subject-Verb). <!-- okay yoda --> <br>
So basically, in English, "I have water" in Əlura would be: <br>:
 (Water I have). 

### Examples
* "wæɹ mi-ɔvæ" | I have water.
    * "wæɹ" is water
    * "ɔvæ" is have
    * "mi" is I or me
* "ðʊ ʊvʊsus ɛjɛi əɹ" | There are two animals.
    * "ðʊ" is two
    * "ʊvʊsus" is animals
    * "ɛjɛi" is there
    * "əɹ" is are
